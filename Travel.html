Travel Spots in India

Agra

Famous for its Mughal architecture and for being home to the Taj Mahal - a monument of incredible elegance and architectural perfection.

Leh Ladakh

Most famous for breathtaking landscapes, the crystal clear skies, the highest mountain passes & thrilling adventure activities.

Andaman

Known for its stunning beaches, rich biodiversity, beautiful marine ecosystem and tropical evergreen forests teeming with wildlife.

Srinagar

Known for the magnificent snow-capped mountains of Himalayas in the background & the vibrant houseboats on the Dal Lake.

Nainital

The charming Himalayan lake town, is a picture-postcard perfect hill-station and one of the most popular in Northern India.

Travel Spots Outside India

Hawaii

Home to sizzling volcanos, world-class rainforests and some of the cleanest, clearest, most pristine beaches in the entire world.

Vietnam

Known for being a popular tourist destination because of its beautiful beaches, it's culture, amazing food (like pho), and friendly people.

Maldives

Known for its clear emerald waters, beautiful beaches that stretch as far as the eye can see, and of course, luxurious overwater bungalows.

Dubai

Famously known for its riches, its glitzy and innovative development and architecture, and its shopping scenes.

Singapore

From shopping frenzy, delectable culinary delights, awe-inspiring museums and exotic gardens - there's something for everyone here!
